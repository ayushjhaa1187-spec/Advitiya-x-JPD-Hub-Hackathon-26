About Smart Link Hub

Smart Link Hub is an innovative link management platform developed for the Advitiya x JPD Hub Hackathon 2026. Our mission is to simplify the way you create, manage, and track your links while providing powerful analytics and automation features.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a free, powerful, and user-friendly link management solution that empowers creators, businesses, and individuals to maximize their online presence.

💡 Innovation

Built with cutting-edge technology including Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and a smart rules engine to automate link management based on your custom criteria.

🚀 Features

From URL shortening to QR code generation, real-time analytics, and custom branded links – we offer everything you need in one comprehensive platform.

🌟 Community

Developed as part of the hackathon initiative to showcase innovation, collaboration, and the power of open-source development.
