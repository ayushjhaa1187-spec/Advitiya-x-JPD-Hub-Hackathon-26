About Smart Link Hub

Smart Link Hub is an innovative link management platform developed for the Advitiya x JPD Hub Hackathon 2026.

Our Mission

🎯 Our Mission: To provide a free, powerful, and user-friendly link management solution that empowers creators, businesses, and individuals to maximize their online presence.

Innovation

💡 Innovation: Built with cutting-edge technology including Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, and a smart rules engine to automate link management based on your custom criteria.

Features

🚀 Features: From URL shortening to QR code generation, real-time analytics, and custom branded links – we offer everything you need in one comprehensive platform.

Community & Hackathon

🌟 Community: Developed as part of the hackathon initiative to showcase innovation, collaboration, and the power of open-source development.

Tech Stack Highlights
  • Node.js + Express backend
  • PostgreSQL for reliable data storage
  • Smart rules engine for advanced redirect logic
  • Modern frontend with a dark analytics-inspired UI
